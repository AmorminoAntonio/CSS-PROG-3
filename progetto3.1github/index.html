Focaccia with Caramelized Onions, Cranberries and Brie

Wow your taste buds with this focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart cranberries and creamy brie. It will become your new favorite holiday starer!

image: Sally Vegas

FEATURED IN:
fresh cranberries recipes that go beyond sauce.

Ingredients

For the Focaccia:

  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 3/4 cups (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus more for the pan
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt

For the topping:

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 t.spoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 t.spoons olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 1 1-2 cuos fresh or fozen cranberries
